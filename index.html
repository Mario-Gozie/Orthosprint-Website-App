ABOUT US

What We Do

At Orthosprint, we believe in transforming lives through innovative prosthetic and orthotic solutions. Our dedicated team combines advanced technology with compassionate care to ensure that every individual receives personalized support tailored to their unique needs. Recently, Orthosprint achieved a remarkable milestone by successfully fitting over 1,000 patients with state-of-the-art prosthetics, significantly enhancing mobility and independence. We are proud to be a trusted partner in our community, helping individuals reclaim their lives and pursue their passions with confidence.

In the last two years, Orthosprint has reduced fitting times by 30% through 3D printing technology, allowing them to serve more patients and improve mobility.

Founder

Meet The Visionary

Chigozirim Oguedoihu

CEO Orthosprint

Chigozirim is a skilled Prosthetist and Orthotist who earned a degree in Prosthetics and Orthotics from the Federal University of Technology, Owerri. With four years of experience, he has worked at notable centers like The Leprosy Mission Nigeria (TLMN) and Human Motion Rehabilitation. In these roles, he provided important care to help people regain their mobility. Chigozirim founded Orthosprint Nigeria to continue his mission of offering high-quality, customized prosthetic and orthotic services. His work reflects a strong commitment to improving the lives of individuals through better mobility and support.

Chigozirim’s inspiration stems from witnessing the profound impact that prosthetic and orthotic services can have on individuals’ lives. He is driven by a passion for enhancing mobility and independence for those who have faced physical challenges. This commitment to improving patient outcomes and fostering a supportive environment motivates him to lead OrthSprint, where he envisions a facility that not only provides high-quality care but also embraces innovative solutions to empower clients in their rehabilitation journeys.

Looking ahead, Chigozirim aims to incorporate cutting-edge technology into OrthSprint, enhancing the effectiveness and accessibility of services. His ongoing studies in Information Technology will empower him to implement innovative solutions that streamline processes and improve client experiences. By leveraging technology, he aspires to set new standards in prosthetic and orthotic care in Nigeria, making a lasting impact on the lives of those he serves and expanding the reach of his rehabilitation services.
