About Us

Empowering Mobility and Independence

At Orthosprint, we are driven by a vision to transform the lives of Nigerians living with mobility challenges. Founded by the young and visionary Chigozirim Oguedoihu, our state-of-the-art prosthetic and orthotic center is committed to providing world-class care and customized solutions to help our clients reclaim their independence. Equipped with the latest 3D scanning and digital design technology, our team of experienced prosthetists and orthotists craft personalized devices tailored to the unique needs of each individual. From high-performance prosthetic limbs to specialized orthotic supports, every solution we provide is designed to restore function and instill confidence. Guided by Chigozirim's personal journey and a deep understanding of our clients' needs, Orthosprint goes beyond just delivering medical devices. We foster an environment of compassion, empowering our clients through rehabilitation and offering the support they need to thrive. As we continue to expand our reach across Nigeria, Orthosprint remains steadfast in our mission to redefine the standards of prosthetic and orthotic care, unlocking the boundless potential of those we serve, one step at a time.

Our Services

At OrthoSprint, we combine cutting-edge 3D scanning technology with advanced biomechanical engineering to create custom prosthetic solutions that perfectly match each patient's unique needs and lifestyle. Our team of certified prosthetists leverages decades of experience alongside state-of-the-art materials and sophisticated gait analysis to ensure optimal fit, comfort, and natural movement patterns. Every prosthesis is meticulously crafted in our modern facility using precision manufacturing techniques and undergoes rigorous quality testing, resulting in durable, lightweight devices that help our clients regain their independence and confidence.

We revolutionize orthotic care through personalized solutions that blend sophisticated biomechanical analysis with next-generation manufacturing techniques, ensuring each device provides unmatched support and functionality. Through comprehensive patient evaluation and digital modeling, our skilled clinicians craft custom braces and supports that harmoniously integrate with the body's natural movement patterns, maximizing stability and rehabilitation outcomes. Built with adaptable, high-performance materials in our advanced manufacturing center, every orthotic device reflects our commitment to empowering patients with greater mobility and confidence in their daily activities.

Founder

Chigozirim Oguedoihu

Founder & CEO OrthoSprint

Chigozirim Nwasinachi Oguedoihu is a dedicated prosthetist-orthotist with extensive experience in human motion rehabilitation. After earning a Bachelor of Technology in Prosthetics and Orthotics from the Federal University of Technology Owerri, Nigeria, Chigozirim worked at the Human Motion Rehabilitation Centre and The Leprosy Mission Nigeria, where he honed his skills in patient education, device management, and collaborative rehabilitation. Currently pursuing a Bachelor of Engineering in Information Technology with a focus on the Internet of Things at Savonia University of Applied Sciences in Finland, he is committed to advancing his expertise and integrating technology into his practice.

Chigozirim’s inspiration stems from witnessing the profound impact that prosthetic and orthotic services can have on individuals’ lives. He is driven by a passion for enhancing mobility and independence for those who have faced physical challenges. This commitment to improving patient outcomes and fostering a supportive environment motivates him to lead OrthSprint, where he envisions a facility that not only provides high-quality care but also embraces innovative solutions to empower clients in their rehabilitation journeys.

Throughout his career, Chigozirim has significantly improved the lives of many individuals in Nigeria through his dedicated services. By assessing clients’ needs and educating them on device use, he has fostered a culture of self-care and rehabilitation. His efforts have led to enhanced patient outcomes, contributing to the overall health and well-being of the community. As a mentor to interns, he has also played a crucial role in shaping the next generation of prosthetists and orthotists, ensuring that quality care continues to thrive.

Looking ahead, Chigozirim aims to incorporate cutting-edge technology into OrthSprint, enhancing the effectiveness and accessibility of services. His ongoing studies in Information Technology will empower him to implement innovative solutions that streamline processes and improve client experiences. By leveraging technology, he aspires to set new standards in prosthetic and orthotic care in Nigeria, making a lasting impact on the lives of those he serves and expanding the reach of his rehabilitation services.

Mission

We strive to provide personalized treatment plans and superior service that empowers individuals to achieve their full potential in movement and daily activities.

Vision

To be recognized as the premier prosthetic and orthotic center that sets new standards in patient care through rapid customization, cutting-edge technology, and accessible solutions, while fostering a supportive environment where every patient feels confident in their journey toward enhanced mobility and independence.
