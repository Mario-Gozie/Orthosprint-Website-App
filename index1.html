ABOUT US

What We Do

At Orthosprint, we believe in transforming lives through innovative prosthetic and orthotic solutions. Our dedicated team combines advanced technology with compassionate care to ensure that every individual receives personalized support tailored to their unique needs. Recently, Orthosprint achieved a remarkable milestone by successfully fitting over 1,000 patients with state-of-the-art prosthetics, significantly enhancing mobility and independence. We are proud to be a trusted partner in our community, helping individuals reclaim their lives and pursue their passions with confidence.

In the last two years, Orthosprint has reduced fitting times by 30% through 3D printing technology, allowing them to serve more patients and improve mobility.
